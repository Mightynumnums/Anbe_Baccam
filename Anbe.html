Anbe Baccam is a NYC based photagrapher. He likes Korean face shops and not wearing socks or a scarf. Anbe hauls from Florida with a single suitcase armed with a camera and enough sass to drive you fucking mad. We love him just for that. Baccam creates beautiful light filled images of people that he finds inspiring. None of his subjects are photoshopped to look like porcelain dolls. Anbe respects truth and individual beauty that is now so rarely found anywhere in the world.
